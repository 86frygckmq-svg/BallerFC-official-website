About Baller FC

Baller FC is a bold, elite football club built on ambition, style, and domination on the pitch. Representing the spirit of competition and the grind to be the best, the club stands for discipline, creativity, and confidence.

Club History

Founded in 2025, Baller FC quickly became known for its strong identity, standout players, and unmatched determination to rise above every challenge. The club continues to grow with the goal of becoming one of the most respected teams locally and beyond.

Founder: Daniel Gomes

Daniel Gomes is the ambitious and driven founder of Baller FC, a football club he started in 2025 with a dream to create something truly special. From the very beginning, Daniel focused on building a team that combines skill, strategy, and passion, while also creating a strong sense of community among players and fans. His vision goes beyond winning games—it’s about inspiring players to push their limits, work hard, and achieve greatness both on and off the field. Under his leadership, Baller FC has grown into a club that stands out for its dedication, teamwork, and unstoppable ambition, reflecting Daniel’s own commitment to excellence.
